Seasonal Trends

Explore how time of year, weather, and day-of-week impact cinema attendance and revenue trends.

Monthly Admissions (2023–2024)

Admissions peak in December and July, aligning with school holidays. Lowest footfall occurs in April and September.

Weather and Cinema Correlation

Rainy and cold days result in higher ticket sales, suggesting cinema is a go-to indoor activity during poor weather.

Day of Week Patterns

Saturdays have the highest attendance, while Tuesdays spike due to discount deals. Monday is consistently the lowest.
